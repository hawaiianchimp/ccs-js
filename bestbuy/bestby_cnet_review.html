      This Sony SLT-A99V DSLR camera features a 24.3-megapixel full-frame sensor to help you capture stunning photos and videos. The 19-point dual autofocus system's 11 cross sensors and a multipoint focal plane phase-detection sensor ensure crisp images.

      Memory card sold separately.

      Product Features

      24.3-megapixel 35mm full-frame Exmor CMOS image sensor

      Captures high-resolution images up to 6000 x 4000 pixels. The charge protection coating on the low-pass filter and image sensor shift mechanism prevent dust accumulation for clear shots.

      Sony A-mount lens mount

      For use with Sony A-mount and Minolta/Konica interchangeable lenses (lenses not included).

      3" Xtra Fine LCD

      With auto and manual brightness control, sunny weather mode, real-time image adjustment, peaking and customization offers a clear view. The 3-way tilting design allows easy positioning.

      High-definition movie mode

      Lets you capture home movie clips in up to 1920 x 1080 resolution with Dolby Digital sound to preserve special moments. The monaural speaker delivers crisp audio with 8-step adjustable volume.

      Up to 10 fps (frames per second)

      So you can easily photograph fast-paced action.

      ISO 100-25,600

      For shooting in most lighting conditions.

      Dual 19-point autofocus system

      With 11 cross sensors and a multipoint focal plane phase-detection sensor with 102 AF points, as well as Translucent Mirror technology, ensure crisp images. AF range control prevents unwanted focusing.

      1,200-zone evaluative metering

      With multi, center and spot options for shooting versatility.

      Color Tru-Finder XGA OLED electronic viewfinder

      With 2,359K dots, real-time camera adjustments, brightness control, diopter adjustment, approximately 0.71x magnification and a 100% field of view helps you frame your shot.

      7 flash modes

      As well as flash compensation, bracketing and metering accommodate a wide range of shooting situations. Red-eye reduction ensures natural-looking portraits.

      Multi-interface adapter

      Allows use with optional HVL-F60M flash with video light and other accessories (not included).

      Quick Navi Pro

      Allows easy 1-handed access to all camera parameter settings. Optional VG-C99AM vertical grip lets you extend shooting time by adding additional batteries (not included).

      Sweep Panorama mode

      Enables you to shoot expansive landscapes by simply pressing the shutter and sweeping the camera vertically or horizontally. Face detection and auto portrait framing let you create stunning images of friends and family.

      Built-in GPS

      Records the location and time of each shot taken, so you can easily view images on a map via your computer.

      Dual media slots

      With support for Secure Digital format in one slot and Secure Digital and Memory Stick formats in another for easy data backup and copying, as well as simultaneously recording movie clips to 2 cards (not included).
