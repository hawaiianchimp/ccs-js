    Description of Services
    Drive Thru Pick-up
    This option is available for our in-stock and special order items. Simply order online and drive to one of our store/warehouse locations to pick up your order. Due to the limited availability and specific qualities of our special order products, NFM must assess a 25% non-refundable deposit. This deposit must be made with a major credit or debit card. We offer an option that allows you to use your Nebraska Furniture Mart Card for the remainder of the balance. To complete an order this way, please call our Phone Sales Team at 800-336-9136.
    Standard Parcel Delivery
    With this option you will receive your package from the US Postal Service in 2-5 business days. We will send you a tracking number so that you can follow the progress of your order.
    UPS Delivery by
    Options displaying Delivery by will be delivered via UPS by the date indicated. With this delivery method, we will send you a tracking number so that you can follow the progress of your order.
    In Home Delivery
    With this White Glove Delivery Service, you will receive a phone call 48 hours prior to your delivery that will set up and confirm a two hour window for your items scheduled arrival. At that time, delivery agents will bring the product(s) into your home, uncrate them, and place them in the area of your choice. They will also remove any of the packaging material from your home. However, this does not include Flooring or the setup of Ready to Assemble products. For guidelines and what to expect, please see our Home Delivery FAQ page. If you have any questions about assembly please call our Phone Sales Team at 800-336-9136.
    Standard Truck Delivery
    When your order has arrived at a local depot, you will receive a phone call to set up a 4 hour window for your delivery date. An adult 18 years or older must be present for the delivery. Upon arrival, a delivery specialist will remove the product from the truck and place it in your driveway or outside the front of your residence. Transporting the item into the home is the customers responsibility. If you live in an apartment, or will have trouble moving the product into your home by yourself you may want to consider upgrading to In Home Delivery.
    Factory Direct Delivery
    With Factory Direct Delivery it will typically arrive in one to five business days after placing your order. It will arrive at your home from one of a few possible small parcel carriers such as (UPS, Fedex or USPS). We will send you a tracking number so you can track the shipment to your home.
