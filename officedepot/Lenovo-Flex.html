Description

  • Unleash your creativity with the Dual-Mode design.  The Flex 2 converts instantly from laptop mode to stand mode and back.
  • Stand mode gives you easy access to the touch-screen display, is supported at a comfortable viewing angle. And what's more, the screen opens a full 300°. 
  • Features a versatile HD (1366 x 768) LED-backlight 15.6" touch-screen display with 10-point multi-touch support.  LED backlighting gives you a thinner, more energy-efficient screen.
  • 4GB of memory runs your programs. 
  • 500GB hard drive holds your songs, photos and documents. 
  • Wireless-N (802.11b/g/n) provides high-performance, cable-free networking and is fully compatible with 802.11b/g.  Access your wireless devices to share files, surf the Web and exchange email. You also have a wired connection available.
  • Features a 2-in-1 memory card for file transfers made fast and easy. 
  • Use the HDMI port to view videos and photos on your HDTV. (HDMI cable is sold separately.) 
  • USB 3.0 port enables ultra-fast data transfers up to 10 times faster than USB 2.0, yet is fully compatible with USB 2.0 devices. 
  • Features a 0.3-megapixel camera. 
  • Built-in numeric keypad speeds data entry and calculations. 
  • Plays and burns CDs and DVDs. 
  • Bluetooth® technology permits short-range wireless data transfers at up to 30' with other Bluetooth-enabled devices, including speakers, printers and phones. 
  • Features Microsoft® Windows® 8.1 operating system. 
  • Stays powered for up to 6 hours.  Battery life will vary depending on the product configuration, product model, applications loaded on the product, power management setting of the product, and the product features used by the customer. As with all batteries, the maximum capacity of this battery will decrease with time and usage.

Lenovo� Flex 2 (15) Dual-Mode Laptop Computer With 15.6 Touch-Screen Display &amp; 4th Gen Intel� Core? i3 Processor, 59425111 5 5 1 1
I like it so far I purchased this at my local Office Depot which has great customer service. This is a new product and it was sold out within a few days so I offered and purchased the store demo at a discount. This is my first Lenovo product and I've only had this laptop for a week but researched that Lenovo has a good quality reputation. I purchased this to replace a 5 year old 17" HP Windows Vista laptop. Here is what I like so far: Very crisp screen resolution, backlit keyboard (low & high), flex feature when using touch screen, seems plenty fast enough for my needs, touch pad works well, does not get hot like my old HP. Best of all it came with 6gb RAM instead of 4 as stated in the specs, it may have just been that particular production run. The only slight con I can think of at this point would be the laptop is a little larger and heavier than some other 15.6" because of the "flex" hinge and the touch screen. I am comparing this to a non-touch ASUS I purchased a month ago for my daughter. This laptop is 1/4" wider and 2" deeper to allow the screen to rotate, and just slightly thicker than the non-touch ASUS. I also compared how well the ASUS and this Lenovo Flex fit in my daughters built-in backpack sleeve. Her ASUS slid in easily while this laptop was a snug fit because of the little larger size, it wouldn't be a deal-breaker for me but something to consider. As far the 8.1 operating system, I can't really see where using a touch screen will come in handy other than when I'm watching a DVD or other online videos. Once I become more comfortable with this 8.1 operating system I may use the touch screen and Aps more but right now it really seems like unnecessary upgrade. July 31, 2014

Overview

  • Budget-friendly multimode computing is here at last in the Lenovo Flex 2 (15 inch), a 15.6” dual-mode laptop with a touchscreen that flips 300 degrees from Laptop to Stand mode. The Flex 2 15” multi-mode laptop is perfect for productivity and ideal for entertainment. With its unique 300° folding design, you can be in classic Laptop mode one minute – and sociable Stand mode the next. Discover a whole new way to work and play with the dual mode design of the Flex 2 15” laptop.

Features

  • At A Glance:

    • Affordable, dual-mode notebook PC – switch between Laptop and Stand modes
    • Unique 300o folding design
    • 10-point multi-touch 15.6” HD (1366 x 768) screen – the convenience of touch
    • Thin and portable – only 1.1” thick
    • Legendary AccuType keyboard
    • Stereo speakers with Dolby® Advanced Audio for an immersive, audio experience
    • One-piece touchpad with cool-touch palmrest
    • Energy Manager – to maximize your time between charges
    • OneKey® Recovery – makes backup and recovery simple

  • Dual-Mode Notebook — Switch Between Laptop and Stand Modes

    When you're relying heavily on the keyboard — drafting documents or working on a spreadsheet, for example — keep your Flex 2 15” in the classic laptop mode for maximum productivity.  But when you're focusing on touch applications, web chatting, or enjoying a film or video, flip the screen 300 degrees to enter stand mode. The Flex 2 15's unique design means it adapts to whatever you choose to Do.

  • 10-Point Multi-touch HD Display

    Interact with your Flex 2 15” by using simple, intuitive gestures; toggle, tap, scroll and swipe, directly on the 15.6" 10-point multi-touch display — optimized for Windows 8.1. Plus, it’s HD at 1366 x 768 resolution, providing brilliant images and graphics.

  • Long Battery Life

    With up to 6 hours battery life between charges, the Flex 2 15” is an ideal companion for users on the go. Lenovo Energy Manager protects the long-term durability of the battery and uses advanced energy-saving technology to increase time spent between charges.

  • Just 1" Thin

    Thin and light laptops usually bear a hefty price tag, but the affordable Flex 2 15” is only 1.1” thick and weighs in at just 5.5 lbs. It’s perfect for the mobile user who needs the dual-mode functionality.

  • AccuType Keyboard

    The acclaimed AccuType keyboard is ergonomically designed to make typing more comfortable and to reduce errors.

  • Stereo Speakers With Dolby Advanced Audio

    The Flex 2 15's speakers deliver rich stereo sound, while Dolby®Advanced Audio provides an immersive audio experience by enhancing audio quality and reducing distortion.

  • Multi-touch Touchpad

    Optimized for Windows 8.1, the intuitive, easy-to-use touchpad features simple scroll, zoom, and rotate functions. You can toggle the Charm bar or app bar, or switch among applications by edge swipe on the touchpad.

  • Simple Maintenance

    With OneKey® Recovery, bouncing back from technical difficulties is a snap. OneKey Recovery makes data backup and recovery simple.
